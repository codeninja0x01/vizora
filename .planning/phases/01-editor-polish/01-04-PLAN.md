   - "Advanced" — any remaining less-used controls (defaultOpen: false)

3. **Guidelines for each panel type:**

   - **audio-properties.tsx**: "Audio" (volume, fade in/out), "Timing" (duration, offset). All expanded by default (audio has fewer controls).
   - **text-properties.tsx**: "Text" (font, size, color, alignment), "Transform" (position/size), "Style" (stroke, shadow — collapsed by default).
